Definitions

Impressions: The number of times an ad is displayed, whether it is clicked on or not.
Impression Share: Actual impressions served divided by total impressions available for your account.
Average Position: A metric showing your overall average ad position (ranging from 1 - 6).
Clicks: The number of times a search user clicks on your Intent Media Ads for Travel ad.
CTR: The number of times your ads were clicked divided by served impressions.
CPC: The average cost-per-click by day when someone clicks on your ad.
Spend: Overall account spend by day.
